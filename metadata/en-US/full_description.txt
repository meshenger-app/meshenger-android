Voice/Video calls on local networks.

True P2P voice- and video phone calls without the need for accounts or access to the Internet.
There is no discovery mechanism, no meshing and no servers.
Just scan each others QR-Code that will contain the contacts IP address.
This works in many local networks such as community mesh networks, company networks or at home.

* voice and video calls
* no accounts or registration
* encrypted communication
* database backup and encryption
* add custom addresses to reach contacts
